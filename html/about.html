About Hemansh

Hi! I'm Hemansh. As a kid, I was always encouraged to find my own canvas of expression. Throughout my life, I have dabbled in many art forms: from poetry to painting, filmmaking, and even podcasting, and have always tried to express myself in various ways. Now, with a bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering and almost two years of practical experience working as a front-end developer in Delhi, I believe I have finally found my canvas.

Future Goals

After lots of research, I think that the right step forward is to become more adept, both technically and aesthetically. For now, my focus is to learn more about User Experience and build more projects using React JS. However, I like to be flexible and await new and intriguing challenges in the future.

Hobbies and Interests

Growing up, films were my choice of escapism. Now with an even more evolved taste, I have become somewhat of a cinephile. I like to watch films from different countries and different languages. 12 Angry Men is one of my favorite classical films.

It's not good to sit at your desk all day. Therefore, I try to go for a walk everyday and listen to some podcasts along the way.
